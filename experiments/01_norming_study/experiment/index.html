Instructions

On the following pages, you will see a series of scenes with a customer and an airline attendant, along with a map of the seats that only the airline attendant can see:

This airline provides customers with two options: either they can pay $50 for a seat assignment or they get a seat assigned at random. Every time a customer pays for a seat reservation, the attendant gets a small bonus.

The following scenes show the customer asking the attendant about their possible seat assignment, to determine how likely it is that they'll get their preferred seat without paying.

After looking carefully at the image, rate how likely you think it is that the attendant would respond with one of two possible sentences. If you think that both options are unlikely, you can answer with a third option, "Something else." To rate the sentences, you will need to distribute sliders so that they add up to 100 points. If you move the sliders so that the ranking totals above 100, then the slider will be pulled back to its original place. Let's look at an example!
